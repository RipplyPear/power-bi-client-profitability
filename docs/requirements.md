# Dashboard requirements

## Objective

Build an interactive dashboard that provides an overview of customer
profitability and behavioral patterns for a fictional retail bank.

## Required KPIs

- average monthly profitability;
- total customers;
- customers with above-average profitability.

## Analysis areas

- monthly transaction activity;
- monthly digital adoption;
- demographics of above-average customers;
- customer product ownership;
- geographic distribution by Romanian county.

## Interaction requirements

- reference-date filter;
- digital-activity filter;
- drill-down or drill-through interaction;
- KPI cards;
- time-series charts;
- demographic composition visuals;
- detailed customer-segment table or tooltip.
